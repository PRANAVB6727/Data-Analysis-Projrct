Soil is the most important part in agriculture. It is the original source of the nutrients that we use to grow crops. That is why, keeping tabs on soil conditions can help a lot in crop management. Data analytics in agriculture can help farmers monitor the health of crops in real-time, create predictive analytics related to future yields and help farmers make resource management decisions based on proven trends. The soil quality is influential on the selection of crops, the need for inputs and for management of the land. Many governments collect soil information in order to gain a better understanding of the environmental conditions in different areas of the country or as part of extension activities.
